# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the VIT Network project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviour includes:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the African tech community

**Unacceptable behaviour includes:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary language
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to **nemesistip@gmail.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
